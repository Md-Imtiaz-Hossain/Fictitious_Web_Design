Introduction

I wanted the website to be equally accessible on phones and desktop. That's why even if I had to design for mobile first, responsiveness was always on the back of my mind. To that end I extensively used flex and @media queries. Since my Fictitious website is a website for a web designing firm, I would say the design philosophy I tried to accomplish was more serious in nature rather than fun / relaxed. That is why most of the time, I tried to make explicit declarations wherever needed.

Breakpoints

I planned to use mainly two breakpoints, mobile (<600px) being one of them. The other one uses a media query of 600px min-width which is for wider screens.

On wider screens, most of the content side by side, whereas on mobile it's all laid down vertically. Most if not all the page follows this simple design decissin in this project.

Menu System (Navigation)

I used the top of the page as a navigation bar which hosts the required links to diffrent pages. On mobile, the navigations are under the company logo, but on desktop site it is justified to the most right using flex.
